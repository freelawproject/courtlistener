Neutral Citations

Beginning on XXX, the Tennessee Court of Workers’ Compensation Claims and the Appeals Board will accept pleadings, briefs, and other filings that include vendor neutral citations to prior decisions as discussed below. All previous decisions have been retroactively assigned such a citation. These citations are created for each decision by the court itself instead of by a third party publisher. As a result, they can be cited immediately when the decision is published by the court.

Format

Citations to the Claims Court take the form:

[YEAR] TN WC [#]

Citations to the Appeals Board take the form:

[YEAR] TN WC App. [#]

In both cases, the format begins with the year, then an abbreviation for the court, and finishes with a serial number indicating the decision number.
