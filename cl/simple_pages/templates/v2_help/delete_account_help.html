Help with Deleting Your Account on CourtListener

It is very simple to delete your account on CourtListener.

When you do, we will immediately delete or anonymize all of the information associated with your account. This may include notes, alerts, tags, and a variety of other data that you may have saved to your account.

After you delete your account you will no longer be able to export your data from your account or restore your account.
